Aster Volunteers in association with Aster Hospitals India are now accepting applications for free paediatric cardiac surgeries for children in need.
This initiative aims to provide life-saving surgeries to children who otherwise might not have access to necessary medical care.

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Children aged 0-18 years with congenital or acquired heart conditions.
  • Families with financial constraints preventing them from affording the surgery.
Application Deadline: 30th September 2024
Application Process:
Submission:
  • Child’s medical reports and diagnosis from a certified cardiologist.
  • Proof of financial status (income certificate, etc.).
  • Identification documents of the child and parents/guardians ( Aadhaar, BPL Card).
  • Photo of the patient with his/her family standing in front of the house.
  • Passport size photograph of the patient
  • Recommendation letter from local Panchayat/ Religious Institution

Review: Our medical team will review the applications and select candidates based on the severity of the condition and financial need.

Notification: Selected candidates will be notified via email or phone. Further instructions regarding pre-surgery evaluations and schedules will be provided.
